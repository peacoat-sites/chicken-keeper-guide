Carol Thompson has kept backyard chickens for 15 years, starting with a small flock of four and eventually managing over 30 birds on a half-acre suburban property. She learned through the school of hard knocks: predator attacks, respiratory illness, molting mystery, and the endless question of why egg production drops in winter. At Chicken Keeper Guide, she writes from genuine experience and real mistakes.

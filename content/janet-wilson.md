Janet Wilson has managed a laying flock of 20 to 40 hens for most of the past decade, with a focus on consistent year-round production and breed selection. She has kept extensive records on egg production rates, temperament, and climate suitability of more than a dozen breeds. At Chicken Keeper Guide, she covers breed comparisons, laying hen management, and getting consistent production from a backyard flock.

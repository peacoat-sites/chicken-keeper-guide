Sarah Mills runs a small permaculture homestead where chickens are a core part of the system, contributing pest control, manure, and eggs in exchange for food scraps and a well-designed space. At Chicken Keeper Guide, she covers integrating chickens into gardens, food forest systems, and small farm operations.
